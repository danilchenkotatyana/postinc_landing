THE POST CONTEST

OFFICIAL RULES

The “Post” contest described herein (the “Contest”) is sponsored by openspot.community Inc., a Delaware corporation having an address of 256 Chapman Road STE 105-4, Newark, New Castle, DE 19702 (“Sponsor”).

ELIGIBILITY:

Subject to these Official Rules, the Contest is open only to individuals who (i) are U.S. legal residents physically residing in one of the fifty (50) United States of America or the District of Columbia; (ii) are 19 years of age or older if residing in Alabama or Nebraska, or 18 years of age or older if residing in any other state or district; (iii) have a registered account on the Sponsor platform (the “Platform”); (iv) have created and published at least one post on the Platform (each, a “Platform Post”), (v) have at least one follower on the Platform; (vi) have sold at least one Platform Post to another user on the Platform; (vii) have shared the official Contest announcement on social media, and tagged and followed Sponsor’s Instagram account (@post.community) and/or TikTok account (@postinc.co) and (viii) are and have been in compliance with Sponsor’s Terms of Service.

Employees of Sponsor, any administrators associated with the Contest, and their respective parents, affiliates, and subsidiaries, and advertising and promotion agencies and any other person or entity involved in the development or administration of the Contest, and their immediate family members or household members are not eligible to participate in or win prizes in the Contest. ALL APPLICABLE FEDERAL, STATE AND LOCAL LAWS APPLY.

ENTRY PERIOD:

The Contest application period begins at 12 AM PT/3 AM ET on March 13, 2023 and ends at 6 PM PT/9 PM ET on June 27, 2023 (the “Entry Period”).

HOW TO ENTER:

During the Entry Period, you will automatically be entered into the Contest if you meet the above eligibility criteria and below Platform Post requirements.

You hereby confirm that: (i) each Platform Post you publish is an original creation and was not copied (in whole or in part) from any other work; (ii) you have obtained a binding written release from (and signed by) every other person or copyright holder who (or whose work is) shown in each Platform Post you publish; (iii) each Platform Post you publish does not infringe or violate any copyright right, trademark right, publicity right, privacy right or other proprietary right of any person or entity (including without limitation, any property owner); (iv) each Platform Post you publish does not contain obscene, lewd, defamatory, inappropriate, disparaging, offensive, racist, homophobic or threatening content; and (iv) Sponsor will not be obligated to pay any compensation to, or permit any participation by, any third party in connection with the publication of any Platform Post via the Platform. If Sponsor determines in its sole discretion that you’ve violated any of the foregoing requirements, you may be disqualified from the Contest.

All non-public personal or other information you provide as part of the Contest will be treated in accordance with Sponsor’s Privacy Policy.

PRIZE:

One hundred (100) winners (each, a “Winner”) will be selected and the following prizes (each, a “Prize”) will be awarded to each Winner and paid via purchase by Sponsor of a Platform Post published by each Winner for such Winner’s Prize amount :

  • 1st place: One hundred thousand USD ($100,000)
  • 2nd place: Twenty five thousand USD ($25,000)
  • 3rd place: Ten thousand USD ($10,000)
  • 4th to 10th place: One thousand five hundred USD ($1,500)
  • 11th to 100th place: Five hundred USD ($500)

Sponsor does not guarantee, and makes no commitments to provide, any prize, benefit or support beyond the Prizes.

Each Winner shall be responsible and liable for all federal, state and local taxes on the value of their Prize.

WINNER SELECTION:

The Winners are automatically selected as the individuals with the highest ratings (“Rating”) based on the following calculation:

  • The Rating for each individual is calculated as the cumulative difference between the number of upvotes and the number of downvotes across all Platform Posts and comments published and/or purchased by the individual during the Entry Period.
  • There is no limit to the number of Platform Posts or comments that individuals can publish or purchase for entry into the Contest and calculation of the Rating.
  • Upvotes/downvotes on each Platform Post are calculated into the Rating for each individual that publishes or purchases the Platform Post. By way of example, if a Platform Post is published by one individual and purchased by another individual, and such Platform Post has 100 upvotes and 0 downvotes, then 100 will be added to the Rating for each individual.
  • The criteria for giving and receiving upvotes or downvotes is determined by Platform users in their sole discretion and may include (but is not limited to) personal preference, aesthetics, and visual appeal of the Platform Post or comment.

In the event of a tie, the winner of such tie will be the individual with the highest total number of upvotes across all Platform Posts and comments published and/or purchased by the individual during the Entry Period, without consideration of downvotes in the calculation. In the event there is still a tie after such calculation, the winner of such tie will be the individual who received such number of total upvotes first in time.
